About Me

I am a sophomore of USC's Bachelor of Science in Computer Science (Games) program. My interests and skills span widely across the visual arts, from traditional drawing and pixel art to 3D modeling and rigging. I am fascinated by the many possible intersections between art and technology, and I intend to continue exploring this with every step of my education.

Outside my creative pursuits, I enjoy music and dance, both as a performer and an audience. I find a sense of wonder in observing the human body move to the flow of various rhythms, and I hope to communicate that feeling through my animated works.

If you have any questions or comments about my work, feel free to send me an email. Thanks for checking out my site!
