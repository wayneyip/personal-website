About Me

I am a junior in USC's Bachelor of Science in Computer Science (Games) program, with a minor in 3D Animation. My interests and skills span widely across the visual arts, from traditional drawing and pixel art to 3D modeling and rigging. I am fascinated by the many possible intersections between art and technology, and I intend to continue exploring this with every step of my education.

Outside my creative pursuits, you'll catch me cooking, singing, dancing, or all of the above at once. I value self-expression, and I view each of those hobbies as a means to do so.

If you have any questions or comments about my work, feel free to send me an email. Thanks for checking out my site!
