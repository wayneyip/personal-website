VIDEO GAMES

Plasticity

Unity · 2.5D Cinematic Platformer · 30-Member Year-Long Project

Role: Technical Artist (Rigging, Pipeline)


I served as the technical artist for Plasticity. Over an entire year, I developed character rigs for puppies, sea lions, and various humans, from joint setup and weight painting to driven key hands/feet setup and facial blendshapes. I also developed a pipeline tool for exporting all animations per character to a single FBX file for project organization. In my spare time, I help troubleshoot Maya issues for our 3D artists.

Winterwood

Unity · 3D Narrative Game · 1 Artist, 1 Engineer, 1 Sound Designer · 10-Week Project

Role: Artist (Character Design, Modeling, Animation)


I was the sole artist for this project, bringing 5 different characters through the full art pipeline: design, modeling, texturing, rigging, animation, and exporting to Unity. I collaborated with our engineer and sound designer on implementation details like walk animation / footstep audio coordination.
