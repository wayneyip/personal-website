Personal Projects

Little Green Soldier

Construct 2 · Action Platformer

Role: Designer, Artist, Programmer (C2 Event System)


I developed this game in my spare time over a span of 4 years, logging my progress and incorporating public feedback on Scirra Forums. I've updated the game more than 10 times with new mechanics, new cutscenes and numerous bug fixes.


Attracted 10,000+ plays across 100+ countries on Scirra Arcade!

God of Music

Unity · High-Score Dodger

Role: Designer, Artist, Programmer (C#)


This game was my first venture into game programming with Unity, forcing me to pick up the interface and C# scripting in 48 hours. Because Unity applies anti-aliasing to art assets, I incorporated third-party solutions to enable pixel-perfect rendering.


Submitted for Global Game Jam 2017.

Chroma Temple

Unity · Dungeon Crawler · 2 Artists, 4 Programmers

Role: Art Lead


For this project I trained our team's artist in pixel art, teaching principles from curve drawing to water and lava shading. I handled the animated ninja, room tiles, waterfalls and torches, delivering over 40 sprites in 24 hours.


Awarded Excellence in Art for USC Newbies and Vets Game Jam 2017.
